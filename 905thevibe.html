What Is 90.5 The Vibe?

90.5 The Vibe Is A Radio Station (Not A Real One) That Operates In Our Discord Server

How Do I Listen?

You Can Listen When It Is On Air In The 90.5 The Vibe Stage Channel

How Will I Know If It Is On Air?

You Will Either Get A Ping Or You Will See Emilia And/Or Studio, Green-bot, Green-bot-2 In The Stage Channel (The Stage Channel Icon Will Be Green)

Can I Request Music?

Yes, You Can! You Just Have To Open The Stage Channel Chat And Scroll To The Top And See If They Are Open And What Songs Are Allowed
